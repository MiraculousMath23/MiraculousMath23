Glossary

Anchor Tag
an anchor tag attaches the link to the page, its also the first tag used for a hyperlink
Attributes
there are multiple attributes and all they do is add to the main tags to give them more detail or alternatives
Division Tag
Separates the body from the head
Hyperlinks
allow you to maneuver around the website and deepdive into topics, connects multiple sites
Line Break
creates a new line on the webpage, breaking upn information into easier chunks
